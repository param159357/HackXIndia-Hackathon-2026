HOW IT WORKS

Six-Step AI Triage Process

STEP 1

CCTV Feed Input

Existing city traffic cameras stream live footage to our secure processing servers. No camera upgrades needed - works with current infrastructure.

  • 24/7 real-time streaming
  • Secure encrypted transmission
  • Automatic failover redundancy
City-wide camera network integration
STEP 2

Vehicle Detection (YOLOv8)

Advanced computer vision identifies vehicles, pedestrians, and their trajectories with 98.7% accuracy using state-of-the-art YOLOv8 object detection.

  • Real-time object detection
  • Vehicle type classification
  • Speed and trajectory analysis
AI-powered vehicle detection with bounding boxes
STEP 3

Violation Identification

AI analyzes detected vehicles against traffic rules to identify violations: wrong-side driving, overspeeding, red light running, illegal parking, and obstruction.

  • Multi-rule violation detection
  • Lane-level precision tracking
  • Weather-adaptive thresholds
Real-time violation identification and classification
STEP 4

Risk Score Calculation

Each violation receives a danger score (0-100) based on multiple factors: vehicle type, violation severity, weather conditions, and location context.

  • Heavy vehicles = +30 risk
  • Wrong-side driving = +50 risk
  • Poor weather = +15 risk

STEP 5

Emergency Override Logic

Emergency vehicles automatically receive infinite priority. The system detects ambulances, fire trucks, and police vehicles, clearing their paths and alerting traffic controllers immediately.

  • Visual emergency vehicle detection
  • Siren audio analysis
  • Route clearance prioritization
Emergency vehicle with infinite priority status
STEP 6

Actionable Alert Output

Priority-ranked alerts delivered to traffic command centers with recommended actions, camera snapshots, and real-time location data for immediate response.

  • Sub-second alert delivery
  • Visual evidence attached
  • Recommended response actions
